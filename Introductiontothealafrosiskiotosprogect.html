The main inspiration for this project
was the film La Chimera by Alice
Rohrwacher. A film full with magical
realism were a man named Arthur, returns
to rural Italy in search for the buried
treasures of the etruscans and a dead loved one.

In a greek review, Arthur is described
as "Alafroiskiotos" in greek literary
meaning "lightfooted" is someone who
can see or percieve things others cannot,
like supernatural entities and in Arthur's
case, know were burried artifacts are
