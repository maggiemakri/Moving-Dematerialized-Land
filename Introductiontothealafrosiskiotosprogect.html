The main inspiration for this project
was the film La Chimera by Alice
Rohrwacher. A film full with magical
realism were a man named Arthur, returns
to rural Italy in search for the buried
treasures of the etruscans and a dead
loved one.
.
.
In a greek review, Arthur is described
as "Αλαφροΐσκιωτος" in greek literary
meaning "light shadowed" and metaphorically:
someone who can see or percieve things
others cannot, like supernatural entities
and in Arthur's case, know were burried
artifacts are before hand.
