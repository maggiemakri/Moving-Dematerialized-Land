“Road landscape” 2024

Mixed media, 109 x 77 cm

A two-dimensional artwork based on the "Road Trilogy" by Wim Wenders

The Journey and the Road, for the films of Wim Wenders are two fundamental elements that work as a metaphor for self-discovery and isolation as well as human connection. In his iconography, a feeling of continuous movement-change unfolds, together with the idea that life is a procedure of searching and adjustment. The two-dimensional environment of the road landscape allows an introduction to the subconscious. The human figures are swinging in and out of reality at the same time. They try to understand why everything changes and nothing stays the same.
